Tepui Tents Open House

Mule Expedition Outfitters Open House.

On April 11th we will be having our first Mule Meet of the year, an open house to welcome the arrival of the Tepui Tents product line. From 10 am - 6 pm we will be open at both our Retail Store and our Install Shop displaying Tepui tents and some of our latest builds. This is a great opportunity to see the Tepui tents in person. If you buy a tent on April 11th it will be 10% off, if we have that tent in stock you could take it home with you.

Find Us Here!

Campfire BBQ will be on hand selling some of their fire cooked smokey goodness.

Come on out with your vehicle and chat with us and your fellow overlanding enthusiasts about what you hope to do and where you hope to go this summer. Also meet the two newest members to our team. Devin Crull who has just started working with Joe and Jon in our Install Shop, and Matt Bell who is working with Dana in our Retail Store.

*We ask that you park at our Retail Store at 98 NE Gilman Blvd and walk over to our Install Shop across Front St at 60 NW Gilman Blvd because of the limited parking near the Install Shop.
